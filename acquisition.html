ACQUISITION

Organizations are constantly striving to build and grow a strong talent pool. Finding and retaining millennial leaders who can lead with tenacity in these disruptive times can be a challenge. Presented below are iCresset’s capabilities in hiring for Board, Executive Leadership & Startup Leadership roles.

Board Re Search

If you are architecting an entire board made up of diverse backgrounds and expertise, our past experience, client focus and presence will turn up the right talent in the most efficient and effective manner possible. Be it CEO or Directors of the Board, we have the network, research capabilities and expertise to manage these assignments with ease.

Executive Re Search

True leaders are rare – capability to spot and discern them from usual loud mouths is rarer! Armed with outstanding Domain expertise in targeted sectors, inherent wisdom to assess the leadership potential and armed with diligently mapped network & processes to head hunt the Best Fit talent for you, we have all the right ingredients to help you experience a new high in Leadership Hiring.

iCresset’s 10 step Executive Search Process

Reaserch & Analysis
1 Industry Analysis

Thorough Industry Analysis by iCresset’s recruiters optimising Sourcing.

2 Client Analysis

Holistic analysis to attract both active and passive candidates. Involves well-honed Sales Pitch backed by a wealth of data and metrics.

3 Job Analysis

Creates strong WIFM for candidates & enables highly targeted shortlisting.

Sourcing & Shortlisting
4 Candidate Sourcing

Explores all possible leads and avenues to find the “Best fit” candidate.

5 Candidate Qualification

Mapping Motivation, Job Fit & Remuneration to achieve high shortlisting and selection ration.

6 Candidate Assessment

Enables Objective Hiring and in- depth candidate analysis through psychometric assessments – A proven approach to reduce attrition and enhance productivity.

Selection & Onboarding
7 Candidate Interview

A thorough briefing to the candidate across all parameters, optimizing prep for the interview and setting them up for a high success ratio.

8 Client Feedback & Debrief

Tested Feedback Loop ensures valuable learnings which are applied for future use.

9 Offer Management

Enabling the “best fit” for both client and the candidate through Expectation Management & Negotiation while working towards a win-win scenario. The process is backed by a scientific Reference Check process.

10 Notice Period & Post Placement

Ensures 100% joining ratio.
